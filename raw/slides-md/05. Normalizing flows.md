_GPT, PixelCNN, WaveNet_ 

**Computes exact likelihood + Multi-modal, flexible model - Slow inference (token-by-token) - Depends on tokenization** 

## **Variational Autoencoders** 

_VAE, ОІ-VAE, VQVAE_ 

**Explicit latent space modelling May serve as building blocks for other models** 

- **+ Interpretability of the feature space + Fast inference** 

## **GANs** 

_GAN, StyleGAN, BigGAN_ 

**Implicit distribution + Fast inference (single step)** 

**+ Photo-realism** 

**- Unstable training** 

**+ Effective representation learning - Blurry images, low realism**

## **Diffusion Models** 

_DDPM, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E_ 

## **Energy-Based Models** _EBM, NCSN, ScoreNet_ 

**+ Exact likelihood computation + Explicit latent space modelling - Requires invertible function - Computational expensive - Good (not exceptional) quality** 

**State of the art Denoising in latent space + High quality image synthesis + Flexible and highly controllable synthesis - Slow inference**
