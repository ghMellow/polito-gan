## **Generative models** 

**Core idea** 

Observed data — images, text, video, graphs — are treated as samples from an underlying probability distribution 𝑝𝑑𝑎𝑡𝑎 

A generative model learns a parametric approximation 𝑝𝜃 ≈𝑝𝑑𝑎𝑡𝑎 

**Formal Goal:** 

min𝜃∈𝑀𝑑(𝑝𝑑𝑎𝑡𝑎 , 𝑝𝜃)

**Parametric Models**

## **Exam** 

- **Group project** (25/30) and an **oral** exam (5/30). The evaluation of the oral exam is individual. 

- Group project guidelines: 

   - Students will work in groups (2-3 students) based on a **self-selected** problem. 

   - **Outcome** : 8-page PDF report in IEEE paper format + code/resources. 

   - Deadlines project proposal:  20/04/2026 

   - Final submission: 7 days before the oral exam, handled via Moodle 

   - Up to +2 extra points will be awarded to projects that propose original extensions, tackle particularly challenging problem settings, or include well-justified and carefully executed experiments.
